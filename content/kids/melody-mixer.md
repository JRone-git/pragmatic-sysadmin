<h1>Monster Beat & Melody Monsters 🎵</h1>

<p>A gentle musical game for kids featuring three modes: an endless runner where you create music as you jump, a step sequencer beat pad for making beats, and a quiz mode to learn your notes by ear!</p>

<h2>How to Play</h2>

<h3>🏃 Mode 1: Monster Beat</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Press <strong>Space</strong> or <strong>Tap the screen</strong> to make your monster jump.</li>
    <li><strong>Land on platforms</strong> to play a musical note.</li>
    <li><strong>Higher platforms</strong> play higher notes, creating a melody as you run.</li>
    <li><strong>Collect Stars</strong> for bonus points and try to beat your high score!</li>
</ul>

<h3>🥁 Mode 2: Beat Pad</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Tap steps</strong> on the grid to create a beat pattern.</li>
    <li>Choose from <strong>8 instruments</strong>: Kick, Snare, Hi-Hat, Clap, Tom, Bass, Piano, and Synth.</li>
    <li>Press <strong>Play</strong> to hear your pattern loop.</li>
    <li>Use <strong>BPM buttons</strong> (80-160) to change the tempo.</li>
    <li>Press <strong>Clear</strong> to start over with a fresh pattern.</li>
    <li>Layer multiple tracks to create full beats!</li>
</ul>

<h3>🎵 Mode 3: Note Quiz</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Choose an Instrument</strong>: Select Piano or Guitar to hear different scales.</li>
    <li><strong>Listen & Learn</strong>: Tap monsters to hear their unique notes, or use "Play Scale" to hear them all.</li>
    <li><strong>Play the Quiz</strong>: Listen to the sound, then tap the correct monster!</li>
    <li><strong>Master the Notes</strong>: Learn 8 different musical notes in a fun, pressure-free way.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Three Game Modes</strong>: Endless runner, step sequencer beat pad, and musical quiz.</li>
    <li><strong>Step Sequencer</strong>: 16-step grid with 8 layered instrument tracks.</li>
    <li><strong>BPM Control</strong>: Choose from 80, 100, 120, 140, or 160 beats per minute.</li>
    <li><strong>Instrument Selection</strong>: Toggle between Piano and Guitar scales for the quiz.</li>
    <li><strong>Soft Audio Engine</strong>: Gentle sounds designed specifically for kids (no harsh noises).</li>
    <li><strong>Educational</strong>: Teaches pitch recognition, beat-making, and note names (C Major & E Major).</li>
    <li><strong>Mobile Friendly</strong>: Works perfectly on tablets and phones with touch controls.</li>
</ul>
