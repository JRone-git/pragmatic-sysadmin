_Last updated: July 1, 2026_

This page explains what happens to your data when you visit [pragmaticsysadmin.help](https://pragmaticsysadmin.help/) (the home of **Pragmatic Tech**) or interact with me by email, chat, or newsletter. I aim to be plain about it — no buried clauses, no surprise trackers. If anything here is unclear, the chat widget in the bottom-right corner is the fastest way to ask.

## What I collect and why

### 1. Analytics (anonymous)

This site uses **Google Analytics 4** (via Google Tag Manager) to understand which articles are read and where visitors come from. GA4 receives:

- Your approximate location (country/region, derived from IP)
- The page you landed on, how long you stayed, and where you went next
- An anonymized referrer (the site you came from, if any)
- Rough device category (mobile / desktop / tablet) and browser

Google sets cookies (`_ga`, `_ga_<id>`) on your browser for this. IPs are truncated by Google before storage. I do not link analytics data to your name or email.

**To opt out:** install a content blocker (uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger), use a browser with third-party cookie blocking (Firefox, Safari, Brave), or visit [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) to manage Google-wide opt-outs.

### 2. The chat widget (Formspree)

When you submit the "Ask Jonne" chat form, your **email address and message** are sent to **Formspree** (formspree.io), which forwards them to my inbox so I can reply. I keep the email until your question is resolved, then delete it on request. Formspree retains delivery logs for up to 30 days for spam/abuse protection, per their own policy.

### 3. The newsletter (Buttondown)

When you subscribe via the newsletter form, your **email address** is sent to **Buttondown** (buttondown.com), which stores it and sends you emails on my behalf. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email, or by emailing me through the chat widget. Buttondown's privacy policy is at [buttondown.com/privacy](https://buttondown.com/privacy).

### 4. The shop (Ko-fi)

When you buy a toolkit or tip via **Ko-fi** (ko-fi.com), Ko-fi processes your payment and collects the information needed to fulfil the order (email, payment details). I never see your full card number — Ko-fi handles that. I receive your email address and the name of the product you bought, so I can email you the download link or updates.

### 5. Affiliate links

Some links on the [Resources](/resources/) page are affiliate links. If you click one and buy, I earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. The vendor (e.g. DigitalOcean) may set a cookie on your browser to track that you came from my site. I do not control these cookies; check the vendor's privacy policy for details.

### 6. Theme preference

When you click the sun/moon toggle in the header, your choice (light or dark) is saved in your browser's `localStorage` under the key `pref-theme`. This never leaves your device.

### 7. Comments, contact forms, and social

There is no comments system on this site and no embedded social widgets. The only ways to contact me are the chat widget, the newsletter, or the social links (if/when they exist in the header).

## What I do NOT do

- I do **not** sell your data to anyone.
- I do **not** run third-party advertising networks (Google AdSense, Meta Pixel, etc.).
- I do **not** use tracking pixels for retargeting.
- I do **not** email you unless you subscribed or contacted me first.

## Your rights (GDPR / EU / UK)

If you are in the EU, the UK, or any jurisdiction with similar data-protection law, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data I hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Receive** a copy of your data in a portable format
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local supervisory authority

To exercise any of these, contact me via the chat widget (bottom-right) or by replying to any newsletter email. I'll respond within 30 days, usually much faster.

## Children's privacy

The [Kids](/kids/) section has simple browser games. I do not collect any personal data from children. The games do not require login, do not save progress to a server, and do not contain tracking. If you believe a child has shared personal data with me, contact me and I will delete it immediately.

## Data retention

- Chat-widget messages: deleted within 90 days of resolution, on request, immediately.
- Newsletter subscriber data: held by Buttondown until you unsubscribe.
- Analytics data: aggregated by Google; raw event data retained by Google for up to 14 months.
- Shop order data: held by Ko-fi per their retention policy; I keep your email only as long as needed to deliver product updates.

## Changes to this policy

If I add a new tracker, tool, or data processor, I'll update this page and bump the "last updated" date at the top. I won't make retroactive changes that affect data I've already collected without telling you.

## Contact

The fastest way to reach me is the **💬 Ask Jonne** button in the bottom-right corner of any page. If email is easier, reply to any newsletter issue and it'll come straight to my inbox.

## Summary for skimmers

- Google Analytics (anonymous, with cookies) — used to know what's worth writing more of.

- Affiliate cookies on the Resources page — set by the vendor, not me.
- A theme toggle cookie in your own browser — only you can read it.
